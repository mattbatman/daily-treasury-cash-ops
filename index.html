What did the federal government spend money on today?

This data is initially prepared and provided by the Bureau of the Fiscal Service in the form of a daily treasury statement, released at 4:00 pm. Treasury.io reformats these statements, giving them a consistent structure, and provides the data as an open source API to the public. Treasury.io is not affiliated with the U.S. Treasury or government.

Note that daily treasury statements aren't issued every single day.
